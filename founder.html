Biography

Born with cerebral palsy (75% locomotor disability), Faheem Ahmed has never let physical challenges define his capabilities. Instead, he has channeled his determination into becoming a passionate technologist, educator, and entrepreneur.

Currently pursuing a Diploma in Computer Science & Engineering, Faheem has demonstrated exceptional academic achievement and a relentless pursuit of knowledge. His journey is a testament to the power of perseverance and passion.

Through his YouTube channel "Tech Classes with Faheem", he has touched the lives of countless students, making complex technical concepts accessible and engaging. His teaching philosophy centers on breaking down barriers and making technology education inclusive for everyone.
