Our Journey

From a simple idea to a transformative platform—discover the milestones that shaped

Skillverse Technologies

2024

The Idea is Born

Faheem conceptualizes Skillverse Technologies as a platform to democratize education and make learning accessible to all.

Early 2024

YouTube Teaching Journey

Launch of "Tech Classes with Faheem" YouTube channel, reaching and inspiring hundreds of students with quality tech education.

Mid 2024

Hackathon Participation

Active participation in hackathons, building innovative projects and connecting with the tech community.

November 1, 2025

Skillverse Portal Launch

Official launch of Skillverse Technologies learning portal on TalentLMS subdomain, marking the beginning of our journey.

Late 2025

First Learners Join

Welcome our first cohort of students, beginning their transformation through accessible, quality education.

Early 2026

Collaborators Join

Miss Shaik Fasiya and Miss Henna Wisal join as collaborators, bringing pharmaceutical and frontend development expertise.

Mid 2026

Independent Website Launch

Development and launch of Skillverse's independent website with enhanced features and user experience.

Future

Global Expansion & Partnerships

TalentLMS API integration, new course launches, strategic partnerships, and becoming a global learning hub.
